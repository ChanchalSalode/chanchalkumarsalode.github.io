Chanchal Kumar Salode

I’m a fourth-year PhD student at IIT Delhi, advised by Prof. Prasanna Ramamoorthy. Currently, my research focuses on operations research and optimization. I build mathematical models applicable to transportation and logistics and design algorithms to solve large-scale optimization problems. Before joining my PhD program, I worked as a research associate at the Indian Institute of Management, Indore, under the guidance of Prof. Saurabh Chandra.
